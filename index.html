Henry Porter is the owner of The Loch Inn, located in the Inverness, Scotland. The hotel offers a relaxing stay, with excellent dining facilities. The Loch Inn offers tours for travellers on vacation. For corporate stays, the inn provides facilities such as free WIFI, a wake-up call service, 24-hour dining, conference rooms, free laundry and pick up services.
