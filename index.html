About Us

Henry Porter is the owner of The Loch Inn, located in the Inverness, Scotland. The hotel offers a relaxing stay, with excellent dining facilities. The Loch Inn offers tours for travellers on vacation. Languages spoken at the hotel: Hungarian, English, German, French, Spanish, Italian The 5-star Loch Inn Hotel is great for guests who are looking to relax, whether on a weekend escape, shopping break or a business stopover. The hotel is conveniently located just three miles from the city centre, so guests can enjoy easy access to the amazing sights of Scotland, as well as a host of spectacular local attractions. Scotland International Airport is less than two miles away, making this hotel the perfect first stop for those arriving in the city.

We have a wide range of facilities at our hotel ranging from lounges and bars to buisness and confrence rooms and of course luxary bedrooms.
